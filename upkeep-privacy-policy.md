**Effective Date:** 7 June 2026  
**App Name:** Upkeep  

Upkeep is a home maintenance app designed to help users manage recurring home tasks, reminders, product records, emergency information, and home-related notes.

Your privacy is important. This Privacy Policy explains what information Upkeep handles, how it is used, and the choices you have.

## Summary

Upkeep is designed to keep your home maintenance information private.

- Upkeep does not require you to create an account.
- Upkeep does not sell your personal information.
- Upkeep does not track you across apps or websites.
- Upkeep does not use your data for advertising.
- Upkeep does not share your home maintenance data with third parties.
- Most app data is stored locally on your device unless you use Apple-provided services such as iCloud backup or device backup.

## Information Upkeep May Store

Upkeep allows you to enter and manage information about your home. This may include:

- Maintenance tasks and reminder schedules
- Task completion history
- Product or appliance information
- Warranty details
- Home notes and records
- Emergency contact information
- Emergency locations, such as water meter or switchboard notes
- Photos or images you choose to attach to records
- App preferences and settings

This information is entered by you and is used only to provide the app’s features.

## How Your Information Is Used

Upkeep uses the information you provide to:

- Display your home maintenance tasks
- Schedule reminders and notifications
- Track completed and overdue tasks
- Store product, warranty, and home record information
- Show emergency guides, emergency contacts, and saved home details
- Personalise app settings and preferences
- Improve your experience within the app

Upkeep does not use your information for advertising, profiling, or cross-app tracking.

## Data Storage

Upkeep stores app data on your device.

Depending on your device settings, Apple may include Upkeep data in iCloud backups or device backups. These backups are managed by Apple and controlled through your Apple ID and device settings.

Upkeep does not operate its own external server for storing your personal app data unless this is added in a future version and disclosed in an updated Privacy Policy.

## iCloud and Apple Services

If your device is configured to back up app data using iCloud or device backup, some Upkeep data may be included in those backups. Apple’s handling of iCloud and device backup data is governed by Apple’s own privacy policies and settings.

You can manage iCloud and backup settings in the Settings app on your device.

## Notifications

Upkeep may ask for permission to send notifications.

Notifications are used to remind you about maintenance tasks, overdue tasks, due-today tasks, scheduled reminders, or daily summaries if enabled.

You can enable, disable, or change notification permissions at any time in your device settings.

## Photos and Media

Upkeep may allow you to add photos to home records, product records, receipts, or similar entries.

Upkeep only accesses photos or images that you choose to add through the app. The app does not access your full photo library unless you grant permission through iOS.

Photos you add are used only to display and manage the records you create in Upkeep.

## Calendar, Contacts, Location, Health, and Financial Data

Upkeep does not intentionally collect or access:

- Your contacts
- Your calendar
- Your precise location
- Health data
- Financial information
- Payment card information
- Browsing history
- Advertising identifiers

If any future version of Upkeep uses additional device permissions, this Privacy Policy will be updated to explain what is collected and why.

## Analytics and Diagnostics

Upkeep does not currently use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools.

Apple may provide developers with aggregated and anonymised App Store analytics, crash reports, or diagnostics depending on your Apple device settings. This information is provided by Apple and does not allow Upkeep to personally identify you.

If analytics or crash reporting tools are added in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated before or at the time those tools are introduced.

## Third-Party Services

Upkeep does not share your personal app data with third-party services for advertising or tracking.

The app may use Apple system services, such as:

- Local notifications
- Photo selection
- App storage
- iCloud or device backup, depending on your device settings

These services are provided by Apple and are governed by Apple’s terms and privacy policies.

## Data Sharing

Upkeep does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Upkeep does not share your home maintenance data with advertisers, data brokers, or external marketing companies.

Your data may only be shared if:

- You choose to export, copy, share, or send information yourself
- It is required by law
- It is necessary to protect the rights, safety, or security of users, the app, or others

## Tracking

Upkeep does not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies.

Upkeep does not use tracking for advertising purposes.

Upkeep does not access Apple’s advertising identifier for tracking.

## Children’s Privacy

Upkeep is not directed at children under the age of 13.

The app is intended for general household and home maintenance use. Upkeep does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so appropriate action can be taken.

## Your Choices and Control

You control the information you enter into Upkeep.

You can:

- Edit or delete tasks
- Edit or delete home records
- Edit or delete product records
- Edit or delete emergency contacts or emergency locations
- Disable notifications in iOS Settings
- Delete the app to remove locally stored app data from your device

Please note that data may remain in Apple-managed device backups or iCloud backups depending on your backup settings.

## Data Retention

Upkeep keeps the information you enter for as long as it remains stored in the app on your device.

You can delete information from within the app, or delete the app from your device to remove its locally stored data.

## Security

Upkeep relies on Apple platform security features to help protect app data stored on your device.

You are responsible for keeping your device, Apple ID, passcode, Face ID, Touch ID, and backups secure.

No method of electronic storage is completely secure, but Upkeep is designed to minimise unnecessary data collection and keep your information under your control.

## International Users

Upkeep may be available in multiple countries or regions.

Because Upkeep primarily stores data locally on your device and does not operate its own user account server, your information generally remains under your control on your device, subject to Apple services and your device settings.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

If changes are made, the updated version will be posted at this page with a new effective date. Continued use of Upkeep after changes are posted means you accept the updated Privacy Policy.
