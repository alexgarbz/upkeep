## What Upkeep Is

Upkeep is a home maintenance app that helps you manage recurring home tasks, reminders, product records, emergency information and home-related notes.

This Privacy Policy explains what information Upkeep handles, how that information is used and the choices you have.

## Information You Add

Upkeep allows you to enter and manage information about your home. This may include:

- Maintenance tasks and reminder schedules
- Task completion history
- Product and appliance records
- Warranty details
- Home notes and records
- Emergency contact information
- Emergency locations, such as water meter, switchboard or alarm code notes
- Photos or images you choose to attach
- App preferences and settings

This information is entered by you and is used to provide Upkeep's features.

## How Upkeep Uses Your Information

Upkeep uses the information you add to:

- Display your home maintenance tasks
- Schedule reminders and notifications
- Track completed, upcoming and overdue tasks
- Store product, warranty and home record information
- Show emergency guides, emergency contacts and saved home details
- Save app preferences and settings
- Improve your experience inside the app

Upkeep does not use your information for advertising, profiling or cross-app tracking.

## Data Storage

Upkeep stores app data on your device.

Depending on your Apple device settings, Upkeep data may also be included in iCloud backups or device backups. These backups are managed by Apple and controlled through your Apple ID and device settings.

Upkeep does not currently operate its own external server for storing your personal app data. If this changes in a future version, this Privacy Policy will be updated to explain what has changed before or when the change is introduced.

## iCloud and Apple Services

Upkeep may rely on Apple-provided system services, depending on your device settings and how you use the app.

For example, Apple may handle:

- Device backups
- iCloud backups
- Local notifications
- Photo selection
- App storage
- App Store analytics and crash diagnostics

Apple's handling of this information is governed by Apple's own privacy policies and your Apple device settings. You can manage iCloud and backup settings in the Settings app on your device.

## Notifications

Upkeep may ask for permission to send notifications.

Notifications are used for task reminders, due-today reminders, overdue task reminders, scheduled maintenance reminders and summaries if enabled.

You can enable, disable or change notification permissions at any time in your device settings.

## Photos and Media

Upkeep may allow you to add photos or images to home records, product records, receipts, warranties or similar entries.

Upkeep only uses photos or images that you choose to add through the app. The app does not access your full photo library unless you grant permission through iOS.

## Data Upkeep Does Not Intentionally Collect

Upkeep does not intentionally collect or access:

- Your contacts
- Your calendar
- Your precise location
- Health data
- Financial information
- Payment card information
- Browsing history
- Advertising identifiers

If a future version of Upkeep uses additional device permissions, this Privacy Policy will be updated to explain what is collected and why.

## Analytics and Diagnostics

Upkeep does not currently use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs or tracking tools.

Apple may provide developers with aggregated and anonymised App Store analytics, crash reports or diagnostic information depending on your Apple device settings. This information is provided by Apple and does not allow Upkeep to personally identify you.

## Third-Party Services

Upkeep does not share your personal app data with third-party services for advertising or tracking.

The app may use Apple system services such as local notifications, photo selection, app storage, iCloud backup or device backup. These services are provided by Apple and are governed by Apple's terms, privacy policies and your device settings.

## Data Sharing

Upkeep does not sell, rent or trade your personal information.

Your data may only be shared if:

- You choose to export, copy, share or send information yourself
- It is required by law
- It is necessary to protect the rights, safety or security of users, the app or others

## Tracking

Upkeep does not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies.

Upkeep does not use tracking for advertising purposes and does not access Apple's advertising identifier for tracking.

## Children's Privacy

Upkeep is not directed at children under the age of 13.

The app is intended for general household and home maintenance use. Upkeep does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Your Choices

You control the information you enter into Upkeep. You can:

- Edit or delete tasks
- Edit or delete home records
- Edit or delete product records
- Edit or delete emergency contacts
- Edit or delete emergency locations
- Disable notifications in iOS Settings
- Delete the app to remove locally stored app data from your device

Some data may remain in Apple-managed iCloud or device backups depending on your backup settings.

## Data Retention

Upkeep keeps the information you enter for as long as it remains stored in the app on your device.

You can delete information from within the app, or delete Upkeep from your device to remove locally stored app data.

## Security

Upkeep relies on Apple platform security features to help protect app data stored on your device.

You are responsible for keeping your device, Apple ID, passcode, Face ID, Touch ID and backups secure.

No method of electronic storage is completely secure. However, Upkeep is designed to minimise unnecessary data collection and keep your information under your control.

## International Users

Upkeep may be available in multiple countries or regions.

Because Upkeep primarily stores data locally on your device and does not currently operate its own user account server, your information generally remains under your control on your device, subject to Apple services and your device settings.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

If changes are made, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact support through the App Store listing or the Upkeep support page.
